# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

As contributors, maintainers, and community members, we are committed to fostering an open, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

Participation in this project should be a positive experience regardless of:

- Age
- Body size
- Disability (visible or invisible)
- Ethnicity
- Gender identity or expression
- Level of experience
- Education
- Socio-economic status
- Nationality
- Personal appearance
- Race
- Religion
- Sexual orientation

We are dedicated to building a welcoming, inclusive, and healthy community from the very first commit onward.

---

## Expected Behavior

We encourage all members to:

- Show kindness, empathy, and professionalism
- Respect differing opinions and perspectives
- Provide and accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Take responsibility for mistakes and learn from them
- Focus on collaboration and the long-term health of the project

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwanted advances
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct deemed inappropriate in a professional environment

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct fairly and consistently.

They may:

- Remove or edit harmful content
- Reject contributions that violate these standards
- Issue warnings or bans when necessary
- Communicate moderation decisions appropriately

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- Project repositories
- Issues and pull requests
- Discussions and forums
- Social media interactions representing the project
- Events, online or offline, associated with the project

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it through the project’s official communication or repository contact channels.

Examples include:

- GitHub Issues or Discussions
- Repository maintainer contact
- Official project email (if added later)

All reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed promptly.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Warning
For minor violations, maintainers may issue a private warning with guidance for improvement.

### 2. Temporary Suspension
For repeated or serious violations, temporary restrictions may be applied.

### 3. Permanent Ban
For severe or ongoing harmful behavior, permanent removal from the community may occur.
